## Design Context

### Users
This is Jannik Wempe's personal portfolio, blog, and CV site. Primary users are prospective clients, employers, and technical peers who want to quickly assess credibility, see proof of work, understand expertise, and decide whether to reach out.

### Brand Personality
Expert, well organized, ambitious. The interface should feel clear, confident, trustworthy, and polished. It should communicate strong technical depth and professional reliability without feeling cold or overdesigned.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light-first. Preserve the current modern personal-brand direction already present in the codebase: Astro + Tailwind, Geist Sans, clean structure, generous spacing, rounded cards, editorial blog readability, and restrained indigo/blue accents with subtle gradients. No strong external references were provided. Favor a professional, high-signal look over playful or overly experimental treatments. Target WCAG AA accessibility.

### Design Principles
- Lead with credibility: make expertise, proof, and contact paths obvious fast.
- Keep it highly scannable: strong hierarchy, clear sections, concise copy, orderly layouts.
- Stay polished, not flashy: use visual interest intentionally, never at the cost of clarity.
- Express ambition through craft: premium details, confident typography, and purposeful emphasis.
- Maintain accessible quality: WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, semantic structure, and inclusive interaction patterns.
